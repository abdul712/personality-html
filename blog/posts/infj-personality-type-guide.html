INFJ Personality Type Guide: The Advocate Counselor

Discover the rare and empathetic world of INFJs and their unique blend of intuition, creativity, and deep emotional understanding

INFJs, representing a mere 1-3% of the population, are distinguished by their introverted, intuitive, feeling, and judging characteristics as identified by the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI). Known for their deep empathy and creativity, INFJs possess a unique ability to understand and navigate complex emotions.

This rare personality type excels in roles that nurture their inclination to help others and foster meaningful connections. Their profound intuition and capacity for seeing the bigger picture makes them natural advocates for causes they believe in, while their empathetic nature allows them to connect with others on a deeply meaningful level.
