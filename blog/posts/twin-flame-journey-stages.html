This comprehensive guide will illuminate each of the eight essential stages, providing you with the knowledge and understanding needed to recognize where you are in your journey and what lies ahead. The chaser typically feels abandoned and confused, unable to understand why their twin would run from such a beautiful connection. They may become obsessive, trying everything possible to convince the runner to return.
