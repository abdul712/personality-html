Managing Social Energy in New Friendships

Building new friendships requires social energy, which introverts need to manage carefully:

  • Schedule Recovery Time: Plan downtime after social interactions to recharge
  • Start Small: Begin with brief, low-key meetups rather than long events
  • Choose Your Settings: Suggest quieter environments where you feel comfortable
  • Be Honest About Limits: It's okay to say you need to recharge or prefer shorter visits
  • Quality Focus: Invest your limited social energy in people who appreciate your authentic self
